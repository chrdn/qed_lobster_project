Boat Selection

Vessel specifications and performance tradeoffs

Subsystem Description

Selecting the right lobster boat involves balancing competing priorities: fuel efficiency, storage capacity, seaworthiness, and cost. This subsystem models these tradeoffs using real-world data to replicate the decision-making process of a commercial fisherman.

Subsystem Modeling

The optimization considers four key variables: hull type, hull length, fuel type, and engine horsepower. All variables are discrete. Inputs from other subsystems help contextualize the problem.
