Subsystem Description

The Fishing strategy Subsystem models the tradeoffs between fishing for different species. This subsystem captures real-world decision-making by incorporating license costs, current market prices, and realistic payment methods.

Subsystem Modeling

The subsystem optimizes across four design variables: target species, crew payment structure, operational hours, and equipment investment. Different target species require different equipment, licenses, work hours, and commute times.
