Lobster Fishing Optimization

class="subtitle">Maximizing profitability through system engineering

Project Overview

This project develops an integrated optimization framework to maximize the Net Present Value (NPV) of a 3-person New England Lobster Fishing operation over its loan term. By modeling the complex interactions between financial planning, vessel selection, and fishing strategy, we identify an optimal configuration for this application.

System Architecture

Three tightly coupled subsystems form our analytical core:

  1. Business Model - Financial viability and revenue streams
  2. Boat Selection - Vessel performance and fuel efficiency
  3. Fishing Strategy - Operational tactics and crew management

Our integrated approach balances competing priorities to maximize long-term profitability while maintaining operational feasibility.
