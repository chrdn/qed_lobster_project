System Introduction

Lobster Fishing Optimization Project

For this project, we are optimizing the parameters for a successful 3 man lobster crew. We separate this system into three subsystems:

  1. Business - Controls the budget and expected profit
  2. Boat Selection - Finds the best boat with respect to fuel cost given the budget
  3. Fishing strategy - Calculates the expected cash inflow based on boat parameters

The Business subsystem incorporates fuel cost into net present value calculations. Fishing strategy provides cash inflow data that the Business submodel uses to obtain net present value.
