System Introduction

For this project, we are optimizing the parameters for a successful 3 man lobster crew. We separate this system into three subsystems: 1) Business 2) Boat Selection 3) Fishing strategy. The Business subsystem controls the budget and expected profit. Boat selection finds the best boat with respect to fuel cost given the budget. This fuel cost is then added to the net present value calculation of the Business subsystem. Fishing strategy takes the boat parameters from Boat selection and calculates the expected cash inflow. This cash inflow is also used by the Business submodel to obtain net present value.
